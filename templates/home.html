The Thing: Infection at Outpost 31 API

In the hidden identity game, The Thing: Infection at Outpost 31, players inhabit characters from John Carpenter's sci-fi cult classic film in a race to discover who among the team has been infected by an alien lifeform that can mimic anyone. Play as one of twelve characters as you lead a series of investigations through the facility using supplies and equipment to clear the building. Infection at Outpost 31 is a large game best played with many people. A digital version solves the problem of having to physically gather with a big group of players. Creating the product would be a taller order for one developer, so I have published an API that delivers all the needed variables and materials for gameplay. This way developers can focus on the front end of their own version.
